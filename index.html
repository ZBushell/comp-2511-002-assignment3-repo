A bit about me

Hi! I'm Zach. It's been way to long for me to have not done this yet, but I finally set up a webpage.

Personal Projects

Mainly the reason why I do University is how much it lets me do with my personal projects. I have 3 that are my obsessions outside of my other obligations. First is my PC. Like vegans (like most vegans) we desktop linux users will shout it from the heavens! I use NixOS it's a suuuper cool distro that lets you reproduce your system configurations based off of one file! know what that means??? Absolutely NO consequences for tinkering! if you pair this with your chosen git remote, you can kiss all those dotfiles goodbye (well not actually but it does cut down the numbers)! As if it wasn't god-tier enough, it now has the calamares installer too. Seriously, try it. Blue screens and broken files will be a thing of the past.
